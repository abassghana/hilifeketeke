It’s a musical event designed to raise funds for aged musicians who are physically ill and inactive due to health issues and also to promote the Ghanaian highlife music to the African communities internationally as a whole as well as resurrect some of our local Ghanaian musicians and their songs.

There will be two highlife musicians with one hiplife supporting artiste who will be performing and touring around Europe which will be held yearly This is expected to cover a period of 4weeks in total as there will be four Schengen states with the musicians touring The event is scheduled to start on the month of March 2020
